PROJECT BACKGROUND

While working on the design of my website, resume and logo, I went through a design process without even realizing it. I wanted to find some way of documenting the key elements of my brand, as well as showcase the process and theory behind these various projects that reflected me. Having seen a few brand books in the past, I thought a brand book would be the perfect way to display the "behind-the-scenes" of my brand. You can click through my brand book above to learn more about the project.
