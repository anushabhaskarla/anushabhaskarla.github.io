VECSTONE Thank You Card

This project was completed for a client looking for a clean and sleek thank you card that embodies their brand. Using the brand's logo, I incorporated the companies existing brand style so that the card would seamlessly integrate with their other materials when communicating with their clients. This modern and minamialistic thank you card enables VECSTONE to build brand value.

Personal Logo

This logo was created using hand drawn digital art. The logo creation was the beginning of my journey to discover my personal brand and incorporates elements that reoccur throughout this website.

Moonset

This was a digital art project I started with the intent of conveying the beauty of a sunset. I decided to replace the sun with a moon because although I appreciate the beauty of a sunset, I feel most like myself at night, when the moon is out. The sky colors are inspired by Avatar the Last Airbender.

INDIAN STUDENT ASSOCIATION

As the Graphic Designer of the Indian Student Association of Indiana University, I was tasked with creating various marketing materials for the organizations events. These events were marketed towards a large audience, and drew up anywhere from 50 to 400 individuals. The design of all marketing was incredibly important, as the organization had been faced with falling attendance for events. Going into this position, I wanted to make some changes to the way that the Graphic Design position interacted with the rest of the organization. Instead of functioning in a silo, I made it a point to interact with other board members and prospective event attendees to get an understanding of what kind of design appeals to our audience. By using a thoroughly outlined design approach that I created, I was able create graphics that sparked interest while also contributing to the creation of the organization's brand. Below are some samples of work from my time in the position. Hover over the images to learn more about the design!

Design Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Wacom Tablet

Garba

I incorporated bright colors to honor the rich culture of Gujrat. The goal of this graphic was to be eye catching, and ideal for print and paper marketing. The goal was to ensure that the poster stands out to a passerby walking down a hallway. Another key point is the incorporation the 'die cut' Garba dancers that embody the nature of the event.

Call Out Meeting

The fonts in the graphic bring out the "school" aspect of our organization, bringing culture and college together. The key point of this design includes a henna printed elephant to emphasize the cultural aspect of the organization. In addition, the organization logo is larger than usual in order to draw attention to the ISA brand as this is the first event of the year.

Diwali Puja

The main focus of this graphic is the diya, which is traditionally representative of this religious festivity. The use the hanging lights and diyas aim to remind ISA members of Diwali celebrations back home. The color scheme of this graphic was inspired by traditional Indian clothing worn during festivals, adding a cultural aspect to the design.

Diwali Night Show

This graphic aims to blend culture and glamour together using various textures and patterns. I hand drew the henna design to represent the cultural aspect of the show and added glitter texture throughout to glamorize the graphic. The Text and image elements were made dynamically to allow for marketing on various platforms.

Back to School Party

Drawing inspiration from concerts and music festivals, this graphic intends to promise a night of excitement and fun. The city scape theme and 'cloud like' gradient markets a fun night out on the town. Following the pattern of traditional concert marketing draws a connection to the kind of experience one would have at this event.

T-Shirt (Product Design)

After communicating with the ISA board, I was able to design an innovative product that captured what it means to be a board member. The paint splatter design is fun and refreshing, something that the board wanted to reflect in the shirt. The feather from the official logo is also embedded into the splatter to add an element of culture.
